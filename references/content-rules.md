# Content Rules

These are the active Xiaohongshu content rules for this workspace.

## Title

- Generate a natural title within **20 characters**
- Do not rely on blunt truncation as the primary strategy
- Prefer semantic rewrite/compression
- For mixed Chinese/English titles, preserve key English terms intact when possible (for example `Anthropic`, `OpenAI`, `harness`) instead of truncating them into broken fragments like `Anthro`
- When a mixed-language title is too long, rewrite the whole phrase into a shorter natural title rather than clipping the English token midway

## Body

- Keep the Xiaohongshu body within **1000 characters**
- Prefer concise, high-signal copy
- Do not pad with repetitive filler

## Image direction

- Prefer relevant real images or watermark-free stock images
- Use lightweight cards only as fallback
- Avoid heavy text-on-image layouts by default

## Tone

- Not too marketing-like
- Emphasize AI information-gap / cognition-gap themes
- Can create mild to medium urgency, but avoid low-quality hype
